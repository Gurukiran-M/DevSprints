Who are We?

NourishUnity - Who Are We

In the spring of 2023, four visionary individuals, Abhinav Bhatt, Dileep Raj, Gurukiran M, and Dipesh, embarked on a journey that would forever change the trajectory of our lives and the lives of countless others. United by a shared purpose and fueled by our passion for positive change, these four second-year engineering students came together to give birth to NourishUnity.


Inspired by the spirit of unity and the call to action set forth by the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal of Zero Hunger, we conceived NourishUnity as a platform to address a fundamental challenge that resonated deeply with each of us. With a collective commitment to eradicating hunger, fostering sustainable agricultural practices, and creating lasting impact, NourishUnity was established.

And with a goal of making the world a better place, the idea for NourishUnity was born.


As a 501(c)3 organization, NourishUnity exists to leverage the power of social media platforms and raise funds to alleviate hunger, homelessness, and unemployment.
NourishUnity will provide life-changing grants, assistance, and both monetary and non-monetary gifts to individuals and families. With your support, NourishUnity will change the world.
